About Us

From Software is a Japanese video game development company founded in November 1986. They are known for their challenging and immersive game design, creating popular titles such as the "Dark Souls" series, "Bloodborne", and "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice". The company has gained a reputation for its high-quality and innovative approach to game development, often incorporating complex narratives and intricate world-building into their games.

  • FromSoftware, Inc. 1986
    Founded in Tokyo, focusing on business application software.
  • First Game Release 1994
    Released "KING'S FIELD (PS)" after PlayStation's launch.
  • ARMORED CORE 1997
    Released "ARMORED CORE (PS)," strengthening our 3D game development skills.
  • FRAME GRIDE 1999
    Entered Sega Dreamcast market with "FRAME GRIDE (DC)."
  • PS2 Market 2000
    Released "ETERNAL RING (PS2)" and "ARMORED CORE 2 (PS2)."
  • Multi-Platform 2002
    Developed for Nintendo GameCube and Xbox, releasing "RUNE (GC)" and "MURAKUMO (Xbox)."
  • TENCHU 3 2003
    Released "TENCHU 3 (PS2)" and published software from another studio.
  • Mobile Gaming 2004
    Entered mobile market with "KING'S FIELD." Acquired Tenchu series from Activision.
  • Banpresto Collab 2005
    Released "Another Century’s Episode (PS2)" with Banpresto.
  • Xbox 360 Market 2006
    Released "[eM]-eNCHANT arM- (Xbox 360)" and "CHROMEHOUNDS (Xbox 360)."
  • PlayStation 3 2006
    Released "ARMORED CORE 4 (PS3)."
  • Demon's Souls 2009
    Developed "Demon's Souls (PS3)" with Sony, winning Japan Game Award 2009.
  • DARK SOULS 2011
    Released "DARK SOULS (PS3)" with Namco Bandai, selling 1.5M units by Nov 2011.
  • Acquisition 2014
    Kadokawa Corporation acquires 80% of FromSoftware's stock.
  • Bloodborne 2015
    Released "Bloodborne (PS4)" with Sony, and "DARK SOULS Ⅱ: SCHOLAR OF THE FIRST SIN."
  • DARK SOULS Ⅲ 2016
    Released "DARK SOULS Ⅲ (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)," selling 3M units by April 2016.
  • DARK SOULS Remastered 2018
    Released "DARK SOULS REMASTERED" (PS4/Xbox One/Windows).
  • Sekiro release 2019
    Released "SEKIRO: SHADOWS DIE TWICE (PS4/Xbox One/Windows)," selling 2M units in 10 days.
  • DARK SOULS Ⅲ Sales 2020
    "DARK SOULS Ⅲ" surpasses 10M units shipped, series exceeds 27M units.
  • ELDEN RING 2022
    Released "ELDEN RING," selling 13M units worldwide by March.
  • ARMORED CORE VI 2023
    Released "ARMORED CORE VI FIRES OF RUBICON."
