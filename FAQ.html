METAL WOLF CHAOS XD

The METAL WOLF CHAOS XD Original Suit pre-order bonus will be released on August 6th 2019 (Tue) at around 12:00PM. Due to this, it will be required that it be downloaded separately via the PS Store.

How to Download:

Start your PlayStation®4 and navigate to the 'PlayStation Store' tab from the Home Screen.
Select ‘Search’ located on the top of the screen and type 'METAL WOLF CHAOS XD'.
Then select the 'METAL WOLF CHAOS XD Original Suit' displayed in the search results located on the right side of the screen and begin downloading.

After selecting a mission, select 'SPECIAL' located on the GARAGE screen and select the METAL WOLF CHAOS XD Original Suit via the 'SUIT SELECT' menu.

METAL WOLF CHAOS XD is available on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC.

DARK SOULS

To access the new areas added through DARK SOULS with ARTORIAS OF THE ABYSS EDITION or DLC, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Defeat 湖獣/Hydra in 狭間の森/Darkroot Basin.
  2. Defeat クリスタルゴーレム（琥珀）/Brown Crystal Golem in 狭間の森/Darkroot Basin and save ウーラシールの宵闇/Dusk of Oolacile.
  3. Defeat クリスタルゴーレム/Crystal Golem at 公爵の書庫/Duke's Archives and obtain 割れたペンダント/Broken Pendant.

If you've already rescued ウーラシールの宵闇/Dusk of Oolacile before your purchase of DLC, start from #3 above. クリスタルゴーレム/Crystal Golem in #3 will drop 割れたペンダント/Broken Pendant only when you have purchased DLC.

COMPANY - Frequently Asked Questions

FromSoftware is a Japanese video game development company known for creating challenging and immersive games, including the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice.

FromSoftware was founded on November 1, 1986.

FromSoftware is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.

Some of FromSoftware's most popular games include the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, and Elden Ring.
