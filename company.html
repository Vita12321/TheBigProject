Our History

FromSoftware was founded in 1986 and released its first game, King's Field, for the PlayStation in 1994. The success of King's Field led to the development of the Armored Core series, which became one of the company's flagship franchises.

In 2009, FromSoftware released Demon's Souls, which garnered critical acclaim and established a cult following. This success was followed by the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, further solidifying FromSoftware's reputation as a developer of challenging and immersive games.

In 2022, FromSoftware released Elden Ring, a highly anticipated game developed in collaboration with George R. R. Martin, the author of A Song of Ice and Fire. Elden Ring was met with widespread acclaim and commercial success.

Our Vision

FromSoftware aims to create unique and engaging experiences for players through innovative gameplay mechanics, intricate world-building, and challenging combat systems. Our mission is to push the boundaries of game design and deliver unforgettable gaming experiences.
